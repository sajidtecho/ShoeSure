Quick Facts on Shipping & Delivery

Shipping

● ShoeSure's shipping service allows you to focus on selling while we handle the shipping and delivery process. With only 18% GST on shipping charges, you can sell your products to thousands of customers, schedule deliveries with access to numerous local couriers, and have the flexibility to set your own prices.

ShoeSure Return Policy

• The ShoeSure Supplier Panel will provide visibility for returns on your inventory. This means you can make adjustments in real-time and deliver a great customer experience and minimize your returns. Use the Panel to manage your returns and reduce your processing costs.

ShoeSure Cancellation Policy

• ShoeSure charges 0 penalties for supplier cancellations and auto cancellations. For situations when a supplier is not able to fulfil an order due to lack of inventory or any other unforeseen situation, suppliers can conduct their business tension-free on Meesho without worrying about the fear of paying penalties.

Return to Origin

• The shipping partner will try three times to reach the customer. If the customer does not accept the product, it will be returned to you. Meesho will not charge a return shipping fee for any RTOs.
